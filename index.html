A Family Tradition of True Italian Craft

"Rooted in the soul of Italy, crafted with the heart of Scarborough."

At Remezzo Italian Bistro, we are passionately devoted to honoring the time-honored recipes of traditional Italian cooking, bringing together the freshest local ingredients and the warmth of an authentic family-friendly dining experience under one welcoming roof. Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the unmistakable love of generations of Italian culinary heritage — crafted to nourish the body, delight the palate, and create memories that last a lifetime.

From hand-rolled pastas to stone-oven pizzas and curated wines, we invite you to gather, celebrate, and truly taste the difference that genuine passion makes. Benvenuti — welcome to the family.

A Menu Crafted with Soul

Each dish is a love letter to Italy, prepared with seasonal ingredients and the finest artisanal techniques.

01

Artisanal Pastas

Lovingly crafted fresh each morning in our kitchen, our hand-rolled pastas feature time-honored recipes — from silky tagliatelle al ragu to pillowy gnocchi in housemade sauces bursting with Italian flavor.

Handmade Daily
02

Stone-Oven Pizzas

Fired in our authentic wood-burning stone oven, our pizzas arrive with classic Neapolitan toppings — San Marzano tomatoes, hand-stretched dough, fresh buffalo mozzarella, and a perfectly blistered cornicione.

Wood-Fired Perfection
03

Premium Wines

Our sommelier-curated wine list features distinguished Italian varietals — bold Barolos, elegant Brunellos, and crisp Pinot Grigios — each thoughtfully selected to complement and elevate every dish on our menu.
