Our Story

Coffee is More Than a Drink

At Brew & Co, we believe every cup is a craft. Founded by two passionate baristas who fell in love with the art of coffee, we have grown into a beloved neighborhood staple where quality never takes a back seat.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships with farmers who share our obsession with excellence.
