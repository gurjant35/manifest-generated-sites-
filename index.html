Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1987

Tony Marinelli opened his first pizzeria on Mulberry Street with nothing more than his grandmother's recipe, a wood-fired oven, and a dream. Today, Tony's Pizza has become a New York institution beloved by locals and visitors alike.

Every pizza is hand-crafted using slow-fermented dough, San Marzano tomatoes, and the finest imported Italian cheeses. We believe great pizza starts with great ingredients and is finished with passion.

  • 100% fresh, locally sourced produce
  • Authentic wood-fired brick oven
  • Original family recipe dough fermented 48 hours
  • Imported Italian San Marzano tomatoes
  • Gluten-free and vegan options available
